  • 通过用户的check-in sequence构建POIGraph
    • Motivation：如果两个POI出现在同一条check-in sequence中，并先后被访问过，就可以在它们之间建立一条边。本质上是利用用户的check-in sequence中POI的共现来获得POI之间的关系。之后利用类似于skip-gram的方法得到node embedding
    • 场景：假如用户已经在时间t之前在若干个POI check-in了，要在当前时刻给用户推荐他有可能玩的POI。POI有side information,比如经度纬度等
    • 收获：可以利用user check-in 的数据构建POI Graph

WWW17

  • Motivation
    • sequential models(such as word2vec) ignore the fact that checkin sequences on different days naturally exhibit the various temporal characteristics.So,the author propose a temporal POI embedding model to not only captures the contextual check-in information, but also various temporal characteristics on different days(weekend and weekday have different pattens, treat a user’s check-ins of one day as a “sentence” not all checkins)
    • incorporate the geographical influence into the pairwise preference ranking method through discriminating the unvisited POIs according to geographical information
    • combining first(temporal) and second(spatial) into a unified model
    • Through constructing the user-POI matrix and measure the correlation of a POI pair regarding the Jaccard similarity of those users who have checked-in at the two POIs, the author finds that the correlation of POIs in sequences is much higher than random pairs which motivate the author to use sequential modeling to capture the relation between POIs.
  • future work
    • consider sequences consisted of consecutive check-ins whose interval is under a fixed time threshold,not necessary a day
    • subsume more information such as users’ comments and social relations in this system to improve performance.
